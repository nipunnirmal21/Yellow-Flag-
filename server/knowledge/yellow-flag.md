# About Yellow Flag

Yellow Flag is a Sinhala Formula 1 podcast created by two passionate F1 fans from Sri Lanka. The goal is to bring the excitement of Formula 1 closer to Sri Lankan fans through race discussions, driver stories, team analysis, and fun motorsport conversations — all in Sinhala.

The tagline: "Sinhala F1 Podcast for Real Racing Fans." Yellow Flag covers race reviews, driver battles, team strategies, Grand Prix reactions, and Formula 1 stories, explained in Sinhala for Sri Lankan F1 fans.

## What Yellow Flag makes

- Race review episodes: raw Grand Prix reactions and honest post-race breakdowns in Sinhala after every race weekend.
- Driver battle discussions: teammate wars, rivalries, and on-track drama explained clearly.
- Grand Prix predictions: bold previews, circuit analysis, and prediction episodes before lights out.
- Fun podcast moments: entertaining banter, fan energy, and memorable clips.
- Fan discussions: live-style conversations, Q&A energy, and community-driven F1 talk.

## The Yellow Flag journey

Yellow Flag started when two F1 fans decided to create a Sinhala podcast for Sri Lankan racing lovers. After the first episode went live, more fans joined every race weekend, building a real Sri Lankan F1 community. Race review episodes became a fan-favourite format after every Grand Prix, and the channel keeps growing with bigger episodes, stronger visuals, and deeper F1 coverage.

## Where to follow Yellow Flag

Yellow Flag is on YouTube (the main home for episodes), TikTok, Facebook, and Instagram. Links to all socials are in the website footer. The website also has the full 2026 race schedule with Sri Lanka race times, track guides, team profiles, live championship standings, and episode highlights.
